pupil_recording_interface.GazeReader¶

class pupil_recording_interface.GazeReader(folder, source='recording')¶

Reader for gaze data.

__init__(folder, source='recording')¶

Constructor.

Parameters
folderstr or pathlib.Path

Path to the recording folder.

sourcestr or dict, default ‘recording’

The source of the data. If ‘recording’, the recorded data will be used. Can also be the name of a gaze mapper or a dict in the format {'2d': '<2d_gaze_mapper>', '3d': '<3d_gaze_mapper>'} in which case the norm pos from the 2d mapper and the gaze point from the 3d mapper will be used.
